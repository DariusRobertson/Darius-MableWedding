Mable’s Bachelorette Retreat

July 22–28

Adventure awaits at a surf hostel in Costa Rica

Welcome to the Bachelorette website that Darius so kindly built us

Why you?

Because you are one of my favorite girls ever. I know you’d be down for an epic adventure and want something new together…

Why Costa Rica?

Mostly to save us all money, flight deals, close beaches, and warm water. We’ll be staying in a hostel with a lush rain forest backdrop, 20–40 mins from the party capital of the Guanacaste region, Tamarindo…

Why July?

Costa Rica’s rainy season goes from June–August; however, we’ll miss the heaviest week of it. Plus this experience says it’s called “Veranillo.” Veranillo is a dry spell that usually starts mid-July, making it the perfect time to go this summer…

Where exactly?

Rapture Surf Camp Costa Rica Avellanas. Your payment to stay here includes breakfast and dinner, along with daily yoga sessions. They also have a pool, air conditioning, and a bar! The camp usually hosts about 15 people so we will take up a decent percentage of the camp during our stay. I wanted to keep almost the entire cost of the trip paid upfront so y’all won’t encounter any surprise costs once we arrive.
